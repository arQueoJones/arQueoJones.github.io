arqueobot – deep intelligence suite

arqueobot is a free, non‑commercial toolkit that integrates all core Python‑based engines from the WAAZP ecosystem. Designed for archaeologists, researchers, and heritage professionals.

⚙️ core modules included:

  • Museum Builder (RL + NeuroEvolution)
  • AI Assistant (GPT4All)
  • Shard / Object Detection
  • Live detection (ESP32-CAM)
  • Multivariate statistical analysis
  • Photogrammetry & COLMAP
  • Classification & training pipeline
  • Hierarchical category management

🔓 License: arqueobot is free for research, education, and non‑commercial fieldwork. Commercial licensing available for enterprise features. The entire codebase is open on GitHub.
